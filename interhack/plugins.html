Plugins

1.x

  • annotations-corpse - display intrinsics/penalties from eating corpses
  • annotations-messages - clarify what certain messages mean
  • apply-oil - press tab to try to apply each potion as oil
  • artifact-name - press tab to abuse artifact naming trick
  • autoadjust - press tab to adjust that item to a particular inventory slot
  • automap - overlay predetermined maps onto your screen
  • automore - automatically space thrugh --More-- after a very short delay
  • botl - define what appears on your bottom lines
  • colors-monsters - color each monster with its symbol's color (e.g. red for fox)
  • confirm-confirm - Really REALLY attack Pakka Pakka?
  • donate - calculate how much you should donate to priests
  • doy-colors-menus - color spells/enhance doy style
  • doy-colors-messages - color top-line messages
  • dual-slow-digestion - tells you when to remove/wear rings to abuse dual SD bug
  • eido-colors-items - color items, Eidolos style
  • eido-colors-status - statuscolors
  • elbereth - lets you press tab for Elbereth at engrave prompts
  • emptylamp-name - name empty lamps empty
  • emptywand-name - name empty wands empty
  • eval - evaluate arbitrary Perl code (do not use!)
  • exp-2nl - changes EXP on botl to list EXP needed for next level
  • floating-eyes - recolor floating eyes to bright cyan
  • force-tabs - forces you to press tab to acknowledge very important messages
  • fortune - random fortune on login screen
  • gem-name - easy gem naming based on hardness
  • geno-list - keeps track of what you've genocided
  • grep - grep your ttyrec with nhgrep (so you get dlvl, turncount, time, etc)
  • hiveminder - easy Hiveminder task creation right in NH
  • hpmon - HP monitor done right (dark gray for pray-safe)
  • idle-gray - gray out idle players in watch-player menu on NAO
  • intrinsics-tracker - keep track of what intrinsics you do and don't have
  • keystroke-count - display count of keys typed so far
  • marker-menu - easy scroll/spellbook name input with a marker
  • mastermind - very quickly solves the castle drawbridge puzzle for you
  • mis-wand-id - attempts to detect when you've messed up your wand engrave ID
  • monster-rename - randomly rename certain monsters for fun
  • mysterious-force - counts the number of mysterious forces you've suffered
  • notes - a notebook for your NetHacking, automatically notes some things
  • powermon - spell power monitor
  • priceid-buy - automatic price IDing (player buying items only)
  • read-spellbook - tells you your odds of reading the seven spellbook levels
  • ring-name - tells you what each sink message means
  • stats - groks your botls
  • strip-menucolors - replace anyone's menucolors with your own (better ones)
  • timer - keep track of your game's realtime
  • unnamed-scrolls - abuse the "named scrolls have 100% chance to write" bug
  • view - view visited-level maps any time
  • wand-name - attempts to name your wands after engrave ID
  • wish-menu - easy (configurable) wish name input

In addition to these plugins, 1.x also has the following built into its core:

  • #rest - rest until HP/Pw fills, or something happens
  • autologin - never have to remember your passwords again!
  • ttyrec playback - for screensavers and debugging
  • local rc edit - don't like the default NAO text editor?
  • ttyrec/key files - automatically record your games/keystrokes
  • no-escape wish - did you really mean to press Esc during wish?
  • macros - key remappings

  • Eidocolors - it's like Skittles for your items

  • TriggerReload - reload plugins on demand
  • Weaponless - helps you maintain weaponless conduct
